ABOUT

I am a software developer with experience in JavaScript, CSS, HTML and Python. Working on creative projects gives me the opportunity to combine technical skill and imagination. I love to simplify life for people and often try to do it with flare.

Over the last two decades, our reliance on technology has increased exponentially. At times we have all found it tiring, particularly when it relates to bureaucratic processes.
That is why it is so important to declutter the technology that we use. Frazzled minds lead to frustrated people. Frustrated people are not good company.
